What is a VPN & why is it required?

A VPN or Virtual Private Network is used by Pernod Ricard,
to allow secure access to Company data, when working outside of any Company Office.
on a standard network comnnection

What is the current VPN software, used by Chivas Brothers?

We currently use a package called BIG-IP Edge Client, otherwise refered to as F5.
If you look in the Hidden Icons area, bottom right hand side of the screen,
if the VPN software is active, you should see an icon. (Icon shown is represntative)
The icon can have 1 of 3 states,

  • Greyed out: Disconnected.
  • Red: Connected.
  • Red with a Flashing padlock: Normally a password issue.

    • State 3 is explained below. However, if you hover your mouse over the icon, it will show Status text.

    When should the VPN connect, and what should I look for if it doesn't?

    The VPN client should automatically connect, when you laptop is connected to any Non Pernod Ricard network, either via wired (cabled), or Wi-Fi (wireless) connection. Laptops should always connect to PR_Employees network, when in a Company site office, no VPN required.
    When working from Home, a coffee shop, hotel or any Public area that has a wired or Wi-Fi connection, the VPN client must always be active, so you can access Company data.
    If you have any issues accessing shared network drives, outside of the Office, the first place to look is in the 'Hidden Icons' area.
    Hover over the 'F5' icon, to see it's text status. If you have changed you Windows password, but not used your laptop outside of the Office, then your will see a Flashing Yellow Padlock on the icon. The 'F5' password does not automatically update, so your old Windows password is still saved.
    Double click on the 'F5' icon, and a pop-up window will appear. Enter your new Windows password into the password box, and make sure the 'Save Password' box is ticked.
