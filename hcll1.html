How to contact the Service Desk

The IT Bar (HCL) Service Desk
UK: 24 x 7 service support

The Service Desk should always be your first contact point, the HCL Team will try to resolve your issue.
However, if they are unable to provide a resolution, they will log a Remedyforce ticket with the correct support Team.
The local support team require a Remedyforce ticket, to be opened before providing support.
