Company Password Policy

The Rules
Expiry Time CBL/CBIL - 90 days, PRUK/GTR - 180 days
Length Min 9 Characters, at least 3 of the below Character types(Allowed/Special)
Allowed Chracters Letters, both Upper and Lower case, numbers (0-9)
Special Characters (Optional) Main ones are !$%*.-_+=
Retention You can't re-use any of your last 8 passwords
Special Rule You can only change your password Once per day

Please think carefully when choosing your password.
Make it secure, but one you can remember.

How to change it?

  1. Make sure you are connected to the VPN, or Office Network.
  2. Press the 'Ctrl-Alt-Del' keys down simultaniously.
  3. Select 'Change Password'.
  4. Enter your old password, and the new password twice, in the boxes indicated.
  5. Click 'OK'.
  6. Wait at least 15 mins.
  7. Repeat step 2 above.
  8. Select 'Lock', this will lock the device screen.
  9. Press a key on the keyboard or move the mouse to display the Login prompt.
  10. Login with your New Password.
